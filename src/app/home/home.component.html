Welcome to The Greene House

Enjoy your stay in our newly-built self-catering lodge. It is furnished in a contemporary style with all modern conveniences and includes an ensuite downstairs master bedroom with amazing views of Lough Foyle, Donegal and Binevenagh (Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty).

Located on the Foyle estuary with access to the Lough Foyle Trail, walks and cycle path. The estuary is a dedicated RAMSAR site and RSPB Reserve and home to the Inch & Foyle Wildfowl Project with Ballykelly Hide being an excellent spot for bird watching. The mudflats and surrounding areas are well-established wintering grounds for brent geese, whooper swans and other migratory birds.

Less than 1 mile takes you to the village of Ballykelly, with supermarket, restaurant, hotel and a selection of tasty takeaways. Just 3 miles away, Limavady (of Danny Boy fame!) is a small market town where you can enjoy the cafes and the unique blend of family-owned independent shops.

The Greene House is ideally located on the Causeway Coastal Route for those wanting to explore the beautiful and rugged North Coast and the historic city of Derry/Londonderry.
